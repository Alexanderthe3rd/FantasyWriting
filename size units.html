Size Units

Note:There is no space between the digit and the unit

  • Absolute Lengths

    • Note: Not recommended to use because of different screen size. can be used for print layout


    1. 1 Centimeters
    2. 10 milimeters
    3. 1 inch

      4. Note:(1in = 96px = 2.54cm)

    4. 10 pixel. In one pixel you wont see much, just a line
    5. 10 points (1pt = 1/72 of 1in)
    6. 1 picas (1pc = 12 pt)

  • Relative Length
    1. 1em. For 2em it means 2 times the size of the current font
    2. 2ex. x-height of the current font (rarely used). 1 ex is too small
    3. 2 ch. width of the "0". 1 ch is too small
    4. 1 rem font-size of the root element
    5. 2vw. 1% of the width of the viewport.1 vw is small
    6. 2 vh.1% of the height of the viewport. 1 vh is small
    7. 2 vmin.1% of viewport's smaller dimension
    8. 2 vmin.1% of viewport's* larger dimension
    9. 100%. % Relative to the parent element
